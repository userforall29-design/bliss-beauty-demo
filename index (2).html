Our Story

A Decade of Beauty
& Confidence

Since 2007, Bliss Beauty Spa has been Rajahmundry's sanctuary for women, men, and brides who deserve the best. We've grown from a small parlour to a full luxury salon and spa — but our commitment has never changed: make every client leave feeling more beautiful and confident than when they walked in.

We use only premium products — Loreal, Schwarzkopf, Wella, OPI, and Lotus. Every stylist is trained and certified. We keep our salon spotlessly clean and hygienic, every single day.
